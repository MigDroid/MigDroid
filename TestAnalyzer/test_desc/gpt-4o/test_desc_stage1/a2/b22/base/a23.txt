Functionality Under Test:
Adding and deleting a task in the ToDo List app.
Interaction Steps:
1. Interaction: Click Skip Button to skip tutorial.
2. Interaction: Click Plus Button to add new task.
3. Interaction: Input 'Sample Todo' Text Field to enter task name.
4. Interaction: Click Okay Button to save task.
5. Interaction: Long Press Task Item to open options.
6. Interaction: Click Delete Option to delete task.
Stop Condition:
Got the observation that a text subject with 'Sample Todo' has Disappear, it is the task name, which means the task was successfully deleted.
